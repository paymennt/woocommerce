Take credit card payments on your woocommerce store using [Paymennt](https://www.paymennt.com/).

== Description ==

Paymennt provides simple, fast and reliable card payment method for the woocommerce stores. It enables Drop-in Frames and Hosted Checkout mode of card payment. 

= Why choose Paymennt? =

Secure payments: Paymennt is PCI Level 1 compliant, and implements world class fraud detection.

Paymennt has no setup fees, no monthly fees, no hidden costs, just pay for what you use, and nothing you don't. No hidden fees even if you take a break from transactions.

Paymennt boasts seamless and robust tech support to its merchants. No waiting in line to get in touch with us.

== Frequently Asked Questions ==

= Who can use Paymennt? =

You need to be registered with [Paymennt](https://www.paymennt.com/) to functionally use this plugin.
[Apply online](https://www.paymennt.com/en/get-started) and start processing payments. Most applications are activated within a few hours.

= What are the requirements for registration? =
Valid trade license, valid identification of shareholders, and a business bank account.

= Do I need to be a registered business? =
Yes, Paymennt services are only available for registered businesses.

= Are there any signup or monthly fees? =

No. you only pay when your customers pay. There are no monthly or minimum charges.

= Is using the Paymennt secure? =

Paymennt is PCI DSS Level compliant for handling card and client data certified. All our transactions are 3D secure enabled to protect you, and our fraud engine aims to capture suspicious transactions before they happen.

= Where can I find documentation? =

For help setting up and configuring, please refer to our [documentation](https://docs.paymennt.com/docs/payment/ecomm/woocommerce).
